RankRush — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 5, 2026 · Applies to the mobile app “RankRush” (store listing name: “RankRush: Math Speed Race”)

RankRush (“the app”, “we”) is a math-racing game with global leaderboards. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and the choices you have. The app is designed to work without any personal registration — no name, email, or phone number is ever required.

1. Information We Collect

CategoryWhat exactlySource / service
Anonymous account ID A randomly generated user ID created on first launch, bound to your device. It contains no personal information. Firebase Authentication (anonymous sign-in), Google LLC
Profile & game data Your chosen nickname, selected country, guild/team name (optional), scores and points, answer counts, daily streaks, trophies/badges, invite code, and season history. Cloud Firestore, Google LLC
App interactions & crash logs Gameplay events (e.g. answers, sessions, feature usage) and crash diagnostics, used in aggregate. Google Analytics for Firebase
Advertising data Device advertising ID, coarse (approximate) location derived from IP, and ad interaction data — only in connection with rewarded ads. Google AdMob
App events Standard app events such as installs and app opens, which may include the device advertising ID. Meta Platforms SDK (Facebook SDK for app events)

We do not collect: real names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location, photos, files, contacts, messages, health or financial information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Core functionality — saving your progress, points, trophies, and streaks across sessions and devices.
  • Public leaderboards — your nickname, country, guild name, scores, and champion history are visible to all other players on the global leaderboards. Do not use a nickname you consider private.
  • Analytics — understanding aggregate usage to improve the game.
  • Advertising — serving optional rewarded video ads (see Section 3).
  • Notifications — optional daily reminders and cooldown alerts, controllable in Settings.

3. Advertising

RankRush uses Google AdMob to show rewarded video ads only. Ads are always started by you (e.g. to refill a life, gain a temporary boost, or save a combo) and are never required to play. Ads may be personalized based on your device’s advertising ID and coarse location, subject to Google’s advertising policies. You can opt out of ad personalization or reset your advertising ID in your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Data is shared only with:

  • Google LLC — Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Google Analytics for Firebase, and AdMob, as described above (Google Privacy Policy);
  • Meta Platforms, Inc. — standard app events via the Facebook SDK (Meta Privacy Policy);
  • Authorities, when legally required, or where necessary to protect users’ safety.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Game data is kept for as long as your anonymous account exists.
  • In-app deletion: Settings → Account → Delete character permanently deletes your cloud data (profile, scores, trophies, season history). This cannot be undone.
  • You may also request deletion by emailing tapwatch.app@gmail.com; we will complete the request within 30 days.
  • Uninstalling the app removes local data from your device but does not by itself delete cloud data — use in-app deletion or email us.

6. Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) between the app and Google’s servers. Access to stored data is restricted by server-side security rules that only allow you to modify your own records.

7. Children’s Privacy

RankRush is intended for users aged 13 and over. The app never requests personal information, and accounts are anonymous by design. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the app, contact us and we will delete it immediately.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your region (e.g. GDPR, CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your data, and to object to certain processing. Because accounts are anonymous, requests are handled per-device: use the in-app deletion feature, or contact us from the device in question so we can identify the anonymous account. You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time in your device settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced in the app. The “Effective date” above always reflects the latest version. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance.

10. Contact

Questions or requests: tapwatch.app@gmail.com
